Mark Twain: The Life and Works

Mark Twain, born Samuel Langhorne Clemens, was an American writer, humorist, entrepreneur, publisher, and lecturer. He is best known for his novels The Adventures of Tom Sawyer and Adventures of Huckleberry Finn.
